About EduTech

Founded in 2020, EduTech started with a powerful mission: to make high-quality, industry-relevant education accessible to everyone. Our founders—experts from tech, business, and education—identified a gap between academic theory and industry practice, and built EduTech to close that gap.

We provide flexible and practical learning experiences to thousands of learners worldwide. With interactive projects, real-world case studies, and expert mentorship, EduTech prepares learners for real challenges in tech, business, design, and other digital sectors.

Today, EduTech collaborates with top universities and leading companies to develop an evolving curriculum. We are committed to empowering personal and professional growth by ensuring that every learner is job-ready, confident, and inspired.
